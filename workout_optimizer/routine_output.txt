# 6-Day Workout Routine

Each session starts with one compound exercise, followed by additional sets to target specific muscle groups.

## Day 1: 4 Exercises
- **Straight Sets (Compound First)**: Incline Bench Press - 5 sets of 8-12 reps *(Chest, Triceps, Front Delts, isometric: Lower Back)*
- **Straight Sets**: Front Raise - 5 sets of 8-12 reps *(Front Delts, isometric: Biceps)*
- **Straight Sets**: Lateral Raise - 3 sets of 8-12 reps *(Lateral Delts)*
- **Straight Sets**: Leg Extension - 4 sets of 8-12 reps *(Quads, Rectus Femoris)*
- **Time Estimate**: 34 minutes

## Day 2: 1 Exercises
- **Straight Sets (Compound First)**: Pulldown - 3 sets of 8-12 reps *(Lats, Biceps, secondary: Lower Traps, Rear Delts)*
- **Time Estimate**: 6 minutes

## Day 3: 1 Exercises
- **Straight Sets (Compound First)**: Stiff-Legged Deadlift - 4 sets of 8-12 reps *(Hamstrings, Glutes, secondary: Lower Back, isometric: Lower Back, Upper Traps)*
- **Time Estimate**: 8 minutes

## Day 4: 5 Exercises
- **Straight Sets (Compound First)**: Incline Bench Press - 2 sets of 8-12 reps *(Chest, Triceps, Front Delts, isometric: Lower Back)*
- **Straight Sets**: Front Raise - 4 sets of 8-12 reps *(Front Delts, isometric: Biceps)*
- **Straight Sets**: Lateral Raise - 3 sets of 8-12 reps *(Lateral Delts)*
- **Straight Sets**: Leg Extension - 4 sets of 8-12 reps *(Quads, Rectus Femoris)*
- **Straight Sets**: Cable Curl - 5 sets of 8-12 reps *(Biceps)*
- **Time Estimate**: 36 minutes

## Day 5: 1 Exercises
- **Straight Sets (Compound First)**: Upper Back Rows - 2 sets of 8-12 reps *(Mid Traps, Rear Delts, secondary: Lats, Biceps, isometric: Lower Back)*
- **Time Estimate**: 4 minutes

## Day 6: 1 Exercises
- **Straight Sets (Compound First)**: Stiff-Legged Deadlift - 2 sets of 8-12 reps *(Hamstrings, Glutes, secondary: Lower Back, isometric: Lower Back, Upper Traps)*
- **Time Estimate**: 4 minutes
